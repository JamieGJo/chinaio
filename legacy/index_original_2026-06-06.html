Phase 1 · Pre-Xi (1980s–2012)

Reform-dominant. China pushed for a "new international political and economic order" (新国际政治经济秩序), emphasising change from within the system.

改革主导。中国呼吁建立新的国际政治经济秩序。
Phase 2 · Early Xi (2013–2017)

Defend emerges strongly. As Xi Jinping promotes his "community of shared future" vision, language shifts toward preserving the existing order against perceived Western violations.

维护立场崛起。习近平推动人类命运共同体，话语转向维护现有秩序。
Phase 3 · Trade war onward (2018–)

Defend peaks in 2021 (84%). After 2022, Reform recovers. The 2025 spike — driven by US unilateralism — marks a record high in defensive language.

2021年维护立场达到峰值（84%）。2025年因美国单边主义再创历史新高。
Classification scheme · 编码方案

What do the stances mean? 各立场的含义

Click a stance to expand its definition. 点击各立场查看定义。

D Defend 维护
Defends the existing international order against challenge or violation — typically by accusing other powers (most often the United States) of undermining rules-based norms, violating sovereignty, or practising hegemonism. This stance treats the current order as broadly legitimate and worth preserving. It becomes dominant from 2013 onward and peaks in 2021 at 84% of classified articles. 维护：捍卫现有国际秩序，通常通过谴责他国（尤其是美国）破坏基于规则的规范、侵犯主权或推行霸权主义。该立场将现有秩序视为基本合法且值得维护。
R Reform 改革
Calls for gradual reform within the existing order — making it more representative of the Global South, more equitable in economic governance, or more multipolar in political terms. Crucially, Reform articles do not advocate replacing the system, only improving it. This is the dominant stance before Xi and recovers after 2022. 改革：呼吁在现有体系内推动渐进改革，使其更具代表性、更公平，或在政治上更趋多极化。改革立场的核心在于不主张取代体系，只主张改善它。
V Revisionist 修正
Advocates overturning or replacing the existing international order with an alternative system — for example, explicitly calling for a "new world order" or rejecting the legitimacy of existing institutions entirely. This is a relatively rare stance in People's Daily, most prevalent in the pre-Xi era and virtually absent after 2014. 修正：主张推翻或取代现有国际秩序，例如明确呼吁建立"新的世界秩序"或完全否认现有机构的合法性。该立场在《人民日报》中较为罕见，主要出现于前习近平时代，2014年后几乎消失。
DR Defend-and-Reform 维护兼改革
Both defends the existing order and calls for reform within it — a common combination in longer analytical or editorial texts that acknowledge flaws while rejecting wholesale replacement. More frequent in Xinhua than People's Daily; the higher rate in Xinhua is partly a text-length artefact (longer articles are more likely to express both positions). 维护兼改革：同时维护现有秩序并呼吁在其内部进行改革，常见于承认现有缺陷但拒绝全面颠覆的长篇分析或评论文章。在新华社中比《人民日报》更为常见。
Key terms tracked · 追踪的关键词

The vocabulary of Chinese international order discourse 中国国际秩序话语的核心词汇

These terms were tracked in People's Daily across the full dataset. Badge colour indicates when the term peaked. Click any term to expand. 点击词条查看释义。

国际秩序 International order All periods
The core search term for this project. Tracked throughout the full archive from the 1980s to 2026. Frequency rises sharply after 2013 and again after 2017. The term is used across all four stance categories, but the balance between them shifts dramatically over time — the central finding of this research. 本项目的核心检索词。贯穿整个档案库（1980年代至2026年）。2013年后频率大幅上升，2017年后再度攀升。该词贯穿四种立场类别，但各立场之间的比例随时间发生了显著变化——这正是本研究的核心发现。
人类命运共同体 Community of shared future Early Xi · Peak 2018
Xi Jinping's signature concept, first articulated around 2013 and massively amplified from 2015. Proposes a vision of global order grounded in shared destiny, mutual benefit, and multilateral cooperation — positioned as China's alternative to a Western-centric liberal order. Enshrined in UN General Assembly resolutions. Usage has remained high but plateaued since 2018. See figure below. 习近平的标志性概念，约于2013年首次阐述，2015年起大幅推广。主张以共同命运、互利共赢和多边合作为基础构建全球秩序，定位为中国对西方中心自由主义秩序的替代方案。已被联合国大会决议采纳。2018年以来使用量保持高位但趋于平稳。
新型国际关系 New type of international relations Early Xi · Peak 2018
An early Xi slogan calling for a new model of great-power relations based on non-conflict, non-confrontation, mutual respect, and win-win cooperation. Introduced around 2013 in the context of China-US relations; later broadened to all international relations. Peaked in 2018 alongside several other early Xi terms and has since declined markedly. See figure below alongside 百年大变局. 习近平执政初期的口号，呼吁建立以不冲突、不对抗、相互尊重、合作共赢为基础的新型大国关系。约于2013年在中美关系背景下提出，后扩展至所有国际关系。2018年与其他多个早期习近平时代词汇同步达峰，此后显著下降。
百年未有之大变局 Great changes unseen in a century Trade war era · Peak 2021
An era-framing term introduced around 2017–18, asserting that the world is undergoing the most profound geopolitical transformation in a hundred years — implying both opportunity and danger for China. Usage surged during the trade war and COVID years, peaked in 2021, and has since declined as the term may have run its slogan lifecycle. See figure below. 约于2017–18年出现的时代定性词汇，主张世界正经历百年来最深刻的地缘政治变革，既蕴含机遇，也潜藏危险。在贸易战和新冠疫情期间大幅攀升，2021年达峰，此后随口号生命周期走向终结而逐渐下降。
走近世界舞台中央 Moving toward centre stage Early Xi · Peak 2018
A triumphalist framing of China's rising global role, prominent at the 19th Party Congress in 2017. Asserts that China is naturally assuming a central position in world affairs. Peaked in 2018 and has since declined sharply — consistent with the broader pattern of early Xi slogans giving way to more defensive language as US-China tensions mounted. 一种宣扬中国国际地位提升的话语框架，在2017年十九大期间尤为突出。主张中国正自然而然地走向世界舞台中心。2018年达峰后急剧下降，与早期习近平时代口号让位于更具防御性话语的整体规律相符。
国际体系 / 战后国际秩序 International system / Post-war international order Rising · new high 2025
Two terms that have surged together since 2022, reaching new highs in 2025. Both are used to invoke the institutional framework built after 1945 — in particular to accuse the United States of undermining the very order it helped construct. The shift from aspirational sloganeering to institutional-defensive language is one of the clearest trends in the corpus. 两个自2022年起同步飙升、2025年创历史新高的词汇。均用于援引1945年后建立的制度框架，尤其是指责美国破坏其亲手构建的秩序。从充满抱负的口号转向制度防御性话语，是语料库中最清晰的趋势之一。
霸权主义 Hegemonism Pre-Xi · resurging 2025
One of the oldest critical terms in the corpus, with roots in Mao-era foreign policy language targeting Soviet "social imperialism" and US dominance. Largely declined in the Jiang/Hu era as China sought engagement, but resurged sharply in 2025 in the context of Trump's second term and US tariff and military pressure. Its return signals a rhetorical register shift back to confrontational framing. 语料库中历史最悠久的批判性词汇之一，根植于毛泽东时代针对苏联"社会帝国主义"和美国霸权的外交话语。在江泽民和胡锦涛执政期间随中国寻求接触而大幅衰退，但2025年在特朗普第二任期及美国关税与军事压力背景下急剧复苏。

About this site · 关于本站

Data and methods 数据与方法

This site presents findings from a systematic content analysis of People's Daily (人民日报) and Xinhua (新华社) coverage of the international order from the 1980s to 2026.

Articles were identified by searching for 国际秩序 (guójì zhìxù, "international order") and thirteen related terms across the full People's Daily archive. Each article was classified for stance using a four-category scheme (Defend / Reform / Revisionist / Defend-and-Reform), developed and validated by the research team.

Corpus size: 9,050 People's Daily articles classified; 1,999 Xinhua articles (cross-validation corpus).

Data sources: paper.people.com.cn (2020–2024), data.people.com.cn (archival), scraped news site (2025–). Some access gaps remain (December 2024 – August 2025).

Status: Preliminary research. Classifications are subject to revision.
